ABOUT US PAGE

NDUOM SCHOOL OF BUSINESS AND TECHNOLOGY(NSBT)

NSBT is a business and technology school as stated in the name.
It is located at Ayensudo,Elmina in the Central region of Ghana.
NSBT is the best dream school for all students that aspire to be big time IT and Business consultants in future.

DEPARTMENTS

IT DEPARTMENT

The Information Technology Department is the department in charge of the study of ICT.
The department has three programmes under it's supervision ;

  1. Information Technology Management
  2. Diploma in Information Technology
  3. BSc Information And Communication Technology

BUSINESS DEPARTMENT

The BUsiness Department is also responsible for the study of all related business programs
